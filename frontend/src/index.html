Company Background

Who We Are

Brilliant Seas Shipping Corporation was established on February 18, 1982. From an initial fleet of two vessels and 29 crew members, it expanded to support general cargo, multi-purpose heavy lift, bulk, tanker, container, inland, and fishing vessels.

Commitment

We are managed by qualified and competent professionals, properly screened and evaluated prior to acceptance, with a continued focus on competency, compliance, and reliable service delivery for global principals.

Recognition

Brilliant Seas is recognized among the top manning agencies in the industry, including POEA Top Performer recognition and citations for sustained operations and contribution to Filipino seafarer opportunities.

Mission and Vision

Mission

"To provide qualified seafarers who are globally competitive, medically fit, certificated and are responsive to the needs of the World Maritime Industry."

Vision
