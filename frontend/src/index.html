HISTORY

GOLDEN INFINITY MANAGEMENT CORP.

Our Company has geared up to establish mutually beneficial tie-up with you. We are a professional company, composed of hardworking and quality- driven people, eager to meet your objective to employ qualified, experienced and competent Filipino ship officers and crew to man your vessels.

Our company is committed to Total Quality Management, with human resource quality control program in place, to ensure you mentorious, fair and honest selection of your men.

There are many advantages to the hiring of Filipino seafarers/Fisherman. Most importantly is their long experience at sea and outstanding record as seamen. Filipino crew has a good command of the English language. Every seaman (officer/rating) has taken his Standards of Training Certification and Watch keeping (STCW) for seafarers in accordance with International Maritime Organizations (IMO) Rules and Regulations.

Filipino Seamen are reliable and hardworking. They maintain a professional and industrious attitude. They are economical to employ but do not compromise themselves on performance or attitude towards their duties and responsibilities. Filipino seaman can be found onboard vessels of all classifications and registries and we believe that they will continue to be an important factor in today's global shipping industry.

Crew and Personnel

Vessel Experience

Crew

Sourcing the best seafarers to ensure that the quality of the personnel we employ meet the standards set-forth by our clients is a fundamental part of our business.

Rigid technical interview and written/computer based screening procedures are strictly implemented during the recruitment process. Checking the candidates' background, health condition, and good command of English is also done prior considering any one for shipboard employment.

GOLDEN INIFNITY MANAGEMENT CORP. maintains a complete computerized database of seafarers - Maritime Personnel System (MPS) A Crew Management Module used to optimize our crew planning and to produce a variety of database generated crew lists, crew on vacation, crew on sick leave etc. Our crew module contains all the data required for entering, view and printing all seafarers, education documentation, training & medical history, previous sea-services reports, etc.

Personnel

GOLDEN INIFNITY MANAGEMENT CORP. Team is composed of seasoned and well-experienced Staff who oversee the selection, administration and crew planning. Their experience in the field of crewing to manage efficiently any challenge that may be faced in the aim to exceed clients' expectations.

Crew administration such as Processing, Crew Claims and Welfare, Accounting and Training are handled by young and dynamic team who satisfies the most challenging demands of the clients, the seafarers' and their families',

We make it a point to follow and keep close contact with our seagoing personnel, and to react immediately to solve any crewing problem.
